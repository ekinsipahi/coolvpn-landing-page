CoolVPN (“we”, “us”, “our”) is provided by {{ COMPANY_NAME|default:"Sipahi Software Ltd." }}. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Summary

  • We do not log your browsing activity, DNS queries, or traffic content.
  • Only minimal account/billing data is kept to operate the service and comply with law.
  • The browser extension applies ad/tracker blocking on-device via DNR. Data is not sold.

Data We Process

Account Data

Email or federated ID, subscription status/plan, invoices and payment status; optional device nickname.

Payments

Processed by third-party gateways; we receive non-sensitive metadata (paid/failed/refunded, currency, plan). We do not store full card numbers.

Service Telemetry (Minimal)

Authentication events (success/fail timestamps), device identifier (seat limits/abuse prevention), aggregate server load (no activity/content ties). Retention is short and limited.

No Activity Logs

We do not collect: websites visited, app content, original IP after connection, DNS lookups, or payloads.

Extension Permissions

  • proxy — to route browser traffic via VPN proxy.
  • declarativeNetRequest — optional on-device ad & tracker blocking.
  • storage — preferences (auto-connect, kill switch, ad-block) & client UUID.
  • tabs (if enabled) — used for per-tab routing decisions; page contents aren’t read.

Cookies & Local Storage

Website uses session/CSRF cookies; extension stores preferences and client UUID locally/sync.

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Contract performance — providing the VPN.
  • Legitimate interests — abuse prevention, security.
  • Consent — optional marketing emails.

Retention

Account/billing per statutory periods; device links while active; security logs short-lived unless needed for incidents.
